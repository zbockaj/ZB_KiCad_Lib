Categorization mostly taken from Octopart, with some things merged.

- IC (MCU, PMIC, logic...)
- Discrete (Diodes, Transistors)
- Passives (Resistors, Capacitors, Inductors, Transformers, Ferrites, Crystals, Antennas)
- Mechanical (Switches, Relays, Buzzers, Heatsinks, Holders)
- Connectors (besides connectors, also includes test points)
- Sensors (Temperature, Acceleration, Magnetic field etc.)
- Opto (LED's, Lasers, Displays, Optocouplers)
